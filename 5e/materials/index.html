FOURTH EDITION NOTE regarding the exercises in CHAPTER 9: Forms:

If you are having trouble with the Form exercise in Chapter 9 (4th edition only), it is because the URL for the PHP script has changed from 'http' to 'https'. The new form element should read:

<form action="https://www.learningwebdesign.com/contest.php" method="post">

The materials for the chapter have been updated to reflect the change.
